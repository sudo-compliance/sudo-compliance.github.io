Profile

Who I am & how I work

I started out fascinated by how systems work. That curiosity turned into a career across IT service management and information security, and I kept getting pulled into the same place: where things break, where risk surfaces, and where someone has to give the business a clear answer fast.

Today I work as a Business Information Security Officer at Barclays, embedded in the Business Banking division. The job is translation in both directions — turning group policy and regulatory requirements into practical action for the business, and turning business reality into risk decisions leadership can stand behind.

Outside the day job I research third-party cyber risk in UK banking for my MSc dissertation and publish open GRC frameworks and tooling on GitHub.
